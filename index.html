SNAKE AND LADDER

Instructions

1.Each player begins with a token or figure on the ‘1’ square in the bottom left-hand corner of the board.
2.Players take turn rolling a die and moving their token in accordance with the number they roll. If they roll a one they move one square; if they roll a two they move two squares, and so on.
3.If a player lands on the lower end of a ladder, they ‘climb’ that ladder to the square that features its top end.
4.Conversely, if players land on the higher-numbered square of a snake, they fall down to its lowest-numbered square. If a player rolls a six, they get another roll after moving their token. To speed up the game, players can use two dice.
5.Whoever reaches the last square first is the winner.
